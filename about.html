About Me

I have spent the past 20 years working in the Health and Fitness industry. During that time I have incorporated a wide-range of specialties and skills into the services I offer my clients. I consider myself a Performance Therapist, which blends fitness specialties, manual therapy techniques, and other healthcare and nutrition related concepts to help individuals improve their performace. I founded my company, Higher Endeavors in 2005, with the mission of helping people achieve their ideal selves.

Early on in my role as a fitness professional, I saw a lack of a comprehensive technological solution to help individuals understand and manage all of the components and variables that are required for them to achieve their ideal selves. I have had many start and stop attempts and creating and implementing such an idea. However, for various reasons, I have never been able to realize my vision. Now, I have taken a more focused approach to realizing my vision and have enrolled in Codecademy's Full-Stack Developer Career Path.

My goal is to become a full-stack developer and create a comprehensive technological solution that will help individuals understand and manage all of the components and variables that are required for them to achieve their ideal selves. I am excited to see where this journey takes me and I am looking forward to the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.
